I am a mixed-methods social scientist and research fellow at UCL. I integrate data analytics with idiographic insights to help organisations understand people in context.

How I Can Help

Evidence Synthesis

I conduct systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and rapid evidence assessments to identify emerging trends, evaluate intervention impact, and translate research into actionable insight. This work supports proposition development, policy design, and behavioural change strategies with a clear empirical foundation.

Data Analytics & Segmentation

Using R and Stata, I build multilevel models, latent class solutions, and segmentations to map audience structure, predict behavioural outcomes, and uncover attitudinal patterns. I also work with survey and social datasets to produce rich, statistically valid portraits of how people think, feel, and act across contexts.

Qualitative & Cultural Research

I design and analyse depth interviews, mobile ethnographies, focus groups, and cultural audits to explore meaning-making, identity, and value. I apply reflexive thematic and interpretive methods to uncover hidden dynamics, generate strategic hypotheses, and bring audience stories to life for design, brand, and policy teams.
