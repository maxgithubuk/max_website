Who I Am

Max Moser is a mixed-methods social scientist working at the intersection of qualitative research and advanced data analytics. His work spans evidence synthesis, multilevel modelling and interpretive inquiry to help organisations understand people in context.

He has led large scale systematic reviews, designed and analysed surveys, and conducted in-depth interviews across a range of policy and clinical topics. Max combines rigorous statistical techniques with an appreciation of the lived experience behind the numbers.

When not analysing data, Max teaches research methods and writes about how psychology and culture shape decision making.
